Common Questions

Can I try before upgrading?

Yes — the Free plan lets you create one full garden plan with basic AI, forever. Upgrade when you need more plans or advanced features.

What happens to my plans if I cancel?

Your plans are always yours. If you cancel Premium, you keep access to view all saved plans, but you'll return to the 1-plan-per-month limit for new creations.

What is the plan archive?

Premium users can archive plans they're not actively working on. Archived plans don't count toward your 7-plan limit, so you can always start fresh.
