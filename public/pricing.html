Common Questions

When does my free trial start?

Your 14-day trial begins the moment you complete checkout. You'll need to enter a payment method, but you won't be charged until after the trial ends.

What happens when my trial ends?

If you don't cancel before the trial ends, you'll be automatically moved to the Monthly plan at $9.99/month. You can cancel at any time from your account settings.

Can I switch plans?

Yes — upgrade to Annual or downgrade to Monthly any time from your account settings. Changes take effect at the next billing period.

Are the printed books extra?

Yes. Your subscription gives you digital access and book creation credits. Printed hardcovers are ordered separately — Annual members get 20% off every order.
