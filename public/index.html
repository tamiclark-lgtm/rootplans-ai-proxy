    ## 1. Garden Concept

    This Zone 7 pollinator and edible garden blends heritage vegetables with native flowering plants to create a productive, beautiful space that works through three seasons. The design layers tall sunflowers and pole beans at the back, mid-height tomatoes and peppers in the center, and cascading nasturtiums and herbs along the front edge — maximizing yield in as little as 4×8 ft.

    ## 2. Recommended Plants
    🍅 Brandywine Tomato ☀️ 🐝🦋
    A rich, complex heirloom prized for its meaty texture and sweet-acidic flavor — one of the most rewarding plants for a Zone 7 summer garden.
    Good companions: Basil, Marigolds, Carrots  ·  Nutrition: Vitamin C, Lycopene, Potassium  ·  🐾 Pet Safety: ⚠️ Toxic to dogs and cats
    🌿 Purple Basil ☀️ 🐝
    A stunning deep-purple aromatic herb that doubles as an ornamental, repels aphids, and produces intensely flavored leaves ideal for pestos and salads.
    Good companions: Tomatoes, Peppers, Zucchini  ·  Nutrition: Vitamin K, Antioxidants, Iron  ·  🐾 Pet Safety: ✅ Safe for pets
    🫛 Dragon Tongue Bean ☀️ 🦋
    A striking yellow-and-purple streaked wax bean that produces abundantly and needs no staking — one of the most beautiful and productive beans for a home garden.
    Good companions: Squash, Carrots, Nasturtiums  ·  Nutrition: Fiber, Folate, Vitamin A  ·  🐾 Pet Safety: ✅ Safe for pets
